Mobile App Design for a Finance Tool

Client FinScope

Category UI/UX Design

Year 2025

Timeline 12 Weeks

Overview

FinScope approached us with a mission to revolutionize how users manage their personal finances. They envisioned a smart, intuitive mobile app that simplifies budgeting, expense tracking, and financial goal-setting. Our challenge? To design an experience that balances robust features with effortless usability.

The Challenge

Finance apps often suffer from cluttered interfaces and overwhelming data. FinScope needed a design that:

  • Simplifies complex financial information
  • Builds user trust through clear visuals
  • Encourages consistent daily use
  • Supports multi-platform consistency (iOS + Android)

Our Approach

We began with deep user research and competitive analysis to understand what real users expect from a finance app. Our team translated these insights into a clean, modern UI that guides users through their financial journey — without friction.

Key focuses:
  • Simplifies complex financial information
  • Builds user trust through clear visuals
  • Encourages consistent daily use
  • Supports multi-platform consistency (iOS + Android)

Key Features Designed

  • Dashboard Overview: A snapshot of spending, savings, and upcoming bills
  • Expense Tracker: Categorized transactions with smart filters
  • Budget Planner: Set monthly budgets with visual feedback
  • Goal Setting: Save for trips, emergencies, or milestones with progress bars
  • Dark Mode: Designed with accessibility and user preference in mind

Outcome

The final design empowered FinScope to confidently launch their MVP and secure investor funding within three months. User testing showed a 40% increase in engagement and a 25% reduction in user churn during the beta phase of this mobile app design project.
